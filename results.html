Ingredients:


18.25 ounces devil food cake mix
5.9 ounces instant chocolate pudding mix
1.0 cup sour cream
1.0 cup vegetable oil
4.0 egg whites
0.5 cup warm water
2.0 cups chocolate chips

Tools:


toothpick, bowl, pan

Methods:


Primary cooking methods: bake
Other cooking methods: beat, mix, batter, dust, stir, cream

Steps:


Step 1:
Ingredients: none
Tools: none
Primary Cooking Methods: none
Other Cooking Methods: none
Time: none
Details/Directions: preheat oven to 350 degrees f (175 degrees c).

Step 2:
Ingredients: vegetable oil, warm water, chocolate chips, devil food cake mix, egg whites, instant chocolate pudding mix, sour cream
Tools: bowl, pan
Primary Cooking Methods: none
Other Cooking Methods: beat, mix, stir, batter, cream
Time: none
Details/Directions: in a large bowl, mix together the cake and pudding mixes, sour cream, oil, beaten egg whites and water. stir in the chocolate chips and pour batter into a well greased 12 cup bundt pan.

Step 3:
Ingredients: devil food cake mix
Tools: toothpick, pan
Primary Cooking Methods: bake
Other Cooking Methods: dust
Time: 50 to 55 minutes
Details/Directions: bake for 50 to 55 minutes, or until top is springy to the touch and a wooden toothpick inserted comes out clean. cool cake thoroughly in pan at least an hour and a half before inverting onto a plate if desired, dust the cake with powdered sugar.
